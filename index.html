AptaCom - Bridging Aptamer Information

More than 3500 aptamer entries

What are aptamers?

Aptamers are small single stranded nucleotides capable of binding with high affinity and specificity to a wide range of targets (from proteins to small molecules)

What is AptaCom

AptaCom is a dataset that compiles more than 3500 aptamers gathered from different sources (i.e. UTexas DB, AptaDB, Aptagend...)

AptaCom is also a XGBoost model trained on the previously described dataset, with the goal of predicting aptamer-protein interactions

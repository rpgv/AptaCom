About Aptamers

Uncovered in 1990, aptamers are single-stranded nucleic acid molecules (DNA or RNA) that can bind with high specificity and affinity to their target molecules.

Aptamers can be applied as a biosensing technology, target drug delivery or as a research tool.

AptaCom bridges information from 6 different sources into a single databases - reaching over 4000 entries.

What are aptamers?

Aptamers are single-stranded nucleotides that can bind with high specificity to their target sequences.

What is AptaCom

AptaCom is a comprehensive collection of more than 3500 aptamers sourced from various sources (UTexas DB, AptaDB, etc).

AptaCom is also a XGBoost model aimed at predicting aptamer-protein interactions.
